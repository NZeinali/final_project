Perth Metro Property Market Predictions

Property market is an attractive sector for many people. The cost of housing expenses is at the top of list of basic living costs. The scale of housing market plays a significant role in the economy. Housing prices are an important reflection of the economy and rising house prices encourage consumer spending and lead to higher economic growth. Housing price ranges are of great interest for home owners, home buyers, real estates, property valuers, home loan providers and developers.

The goal of this project is to employ machine learning techniques to create a regression model to estimate the price of the house given specific features. Some data visualisation is also provided to understand the insight of the data.
